About Us

Nesma Holdings is a diversified investment and services company committed to delivering sustainable value across a broad range of industries. With a strong foundation built on integrity, innovation, and operational excellence, the company partners with leading global and regional organizations to drive growth and long-term success. Through its portfolio of businesses, Nesma Holdings operates across sectors including construction, logistics, energy, engineering, and technology. The company is dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and performance, ensuring that every project and service contributes positively to clients, communities, and stakeholders. At the core of Nesma Holdings’ philosophy is a commitment to people and progress, investing in talent, fostering strategic partnerships, and embracing forward-thinking solutions to support economic development and a resilient future.
